    Reddit sync

    When you mark a Reddit item Done, it's queued to be unsaved from your Reddit Saved list. A scheduled job (or the button below) does the unsaving, so triage stays instant and nothing is lost if your cookie expires.

    Checking...

    Get it in your browser: DevTools → Application → Cookies → reddit.com → reddit_session. It expires every few days — re-paste it when sync reports the session expired.

    Import content

    Add saved content from a file, or paste a YouTube playlist/video URL. Re-importing is non-destructive — your triage progress is never overwritten.

    • Reddit — reddit-saved-manager app.db, or a .csv/.json export
    • YouTube — a playlist/video URL below, or a yt-dlp --flat-playlist JSON file
    • Hacker News — Materialistic .db, or a .txt list of story IDs
    • Obsidian / Keep — whole folders: use the import CLI (browsers can't upload folders)
